5 Days Goa Tour Package from Gorakhpur

Goa Beach & Leisure Holiday

Pickup & Drop: Gorakhpur Railway Station / City
5 DAYS

Tour Overview

Enjoy a perfect blend of beaches, nightlife, culture, and relaxation with TheKindredTravels. This 5 Days Goa Tour Package is ideal for couples, families, and friends looking for a refreshing coastal holiday.

Tour Highlights:

  • North & South Goa sightseeing
  • Famous beaches like Baga, Calangute & Palolem
  • Churches & heritage sites of Old Goa
  • Optional water sports & nightlife experience
  • Comfortable hotel stay with breakfast

Day 01: Arrival in Goa

Upon arrival at Goa Airport or Railway Station, meet our representative and transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Check in and relax. The rest of the day is free to explore nearby beaches or unwind at leisure. Overnight stay in Goa.

Day 02: North Goa Sightseeing Tour

After breakfast, proceed for a full-day North Goa sightseeing tour. Visit popular attractions including Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, Anjuna Beach, and Fort Aguada. Explore vibrant local markets and enjoy Goa’s lively beach atmosphere. Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 03: South Goa Sightseeing Tour

Post breakfast, head out for South Goa sightseeing. Visit the serene Colva Beach, Miramar Beach, Dona Paula viewpoint, and explore the historic churches of Old Goa including the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Goa.

Day 04: Leisure Day / Optional Water Sports

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. This day is reserved for leisure or optional water sports such as parasailing, jet skiing, banana boat rides, and other beach activities (available at additional cost). Relax and soak in the coastal charm of Goa. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05: Departure from Goa

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Goa Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, carrying wonderful memories of your Goa holiday.
