3 Days Agra Tour Package from Gorakhpur

Agra Heritage & Mughal Tour

Pickup & Drop: Gorakhpur Railway Station / City
3 DAYS

Tour Overview

Explore India’s most iconic heritage destination with TheKindredTravels. This 3 Days Agra Tour Package from Gorakhpur is perfect for families, couples, and history lovers.

Tour Highlights:

  • Comfortable road journey from Gorakhpur to Agra
  • Visit the world-famous Taj Mahal
  • Explore Agra Fort & Fatehpur Sikri
  • Ideal short heritage getaway

Day 01: Gorakhpur → Agra

Early morning pickup from Gorakhpur Railway Station / City. Drive towards Agra via comfortable road journey. Evening hotel check-in and rest.

Day 02: Agra Sightseeing

Visit the iconic Taj Mahal at sunrise. Explore Agra Fort and enjoy local sightseeing. Optional visit to Mehtab Bagh.

Day 03: Fatehpur Sikri → Gorakhpur

After breakfast, visit Fatehpur Sikri. Start return journey to Gorakhpur. Drop-off by evening.
